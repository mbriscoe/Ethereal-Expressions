Ethereal Expressions

From Paris to New York both, Adamma's art captivated critics, artists and public. Her figure has represented a fresh breeze for the artistic sphere.

Using the impasto technique with a delicious palette, Adamma managed to capture the beauty of the Welsh coast and Coutryside, the silky textures of the Nigerian Gele and the vibrant and exquisite colours of Nigerian Iborum, Buba and Iro.

When art belongs to the observer, it becomes the most extraordinary form of catharsis
